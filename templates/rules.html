SuperMorpion

The game is composed by 91 cases. Each block of 9 cases, from now on reffered as a big case, is a classic tic-tac-toe. The group of the 9 big cases is itself a tic-tac-toe.

In order to win, the player must complete a line of 3 big cases (horizontally, vertically or diagonally). In order to win a big case, a player must complete a line of 3 cases (horizontally, vertically or diagonally) inside the big case.

The X player plays first. He must play in the central big case. Then the O player must play in the big case corresponding to the small case X has played (eg if X plays in the middle-left case of the central big case, them the O player must play in te middle-left big case). A player cannot play in a complete big case, so in the case the player should play in such a big case, he can play in any big case.
